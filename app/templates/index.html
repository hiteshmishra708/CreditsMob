FAQs
  • Q: How am I getting rewards without paying anything?
    Creditsmob has partnered with companies who want you to use their products. These products are primarily mobile applications. So, they pay Creditsmob a fee for every app download, and we share that with you in the form of a reward!
  • Q: What data do you collect?
    A: All we need is your Device Identifier and Email Address. The Device Identifier is an anonymous ID that we use to track the number of credits you receive. This identifier is supplied by the phone manufacturer and does not tell us who you are. The Email Address is used to send rewards once you have redeemed them. We don’t collect or store any other information.
  • I downloaded an app, but did not get any credits?
    A: Make sure to launch the app and use it for at least a few minutes. Only then do we receive confirmation from our partners that you have successfully used the app, and we can update your credits.
  • Q: I launched the app, used it, but still no credits?
    A: Sometimes the notification from our partners might be a bit delayed. Refresh the page/close the app/ wait for a few minutes. If it still does not work, please write to us at support@creditsmob.com.
  • I redeemed the reward, but have not got it via email?
    A: Rewards will be sent to you within 48 hours from when you request them. If you don’t receive it within that time, please write to us at support@creditsmob.com.
  • Q: Why do I not see any offers on the home page?
    A: This is probably because you have tried out all the apps/offers we have available. We are continually working to get more partners so we can get you more offers. You can help us by using the already downloaded apps more frequently. This way our partners will value the quality of users we are providing them.
  • Q: Still need help?
    A: Please reach out to support@creditsmob.com and we'll be happy to help.
  • Q: How do I make changes to my Email Address/Facebook account?
    A: Please write to us at support@creditsmob.com and we’ll make the changes for you.
